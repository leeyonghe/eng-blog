# 📚 **매일 배우는 고급 영단어 #233**

## 🌟 **오늘의 단어 (11월 09일)**

### **Resilient** /rɪˈzɪliənt/

**한국어 뜻**: 회복력이 있는, 탄력적인  
**영어 정의**: Able to recover quickly from difficulties

<!--more-->

---

## 📖 **예문 (Examples)**

**1.** *Children are remarkably resilient in the face of adversity.*

**2.** *The resilient economy bounced back from the recession.*

**동의어 (Synonyms)**: adaptable, flexible, robust

**어족 (Word Family)**:
- Noun: resilience
- Adverb: resiliently
